Thermal Paste

What is Thermal Paste?

Thermal Paste is a material used to improve heat transfer between a CPU (processor) and a heat sink.

Examples:

  • Thermal paste is used for improving heat transfer. (used for + -ing)
  • Thermal paste is used to reduce the temperature of components. (used to + verb)

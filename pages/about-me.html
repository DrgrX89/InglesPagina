3D Printer

What is a 3D Printer?

A 3D Printer is a machine used to create three-dimensional objects from digital models.

Examples:

  • A 3D printer is used for creating 3D objects. (used for + -ing)
  • A 3D printer is used to print prototypes. (used to + verb)
